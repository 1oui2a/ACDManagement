ACD Management: Independent Event & Artist Manager

ACD management aids independent artists in hosting gigs and events to showcase their music in venues with packed audiences, specialising in the Dublin area.

ACD management has put on plenty of successful shows, such as:

  • Ladies First, ladies first is a run of shows that take place in the grand social throughout the year. This gig celebrates women and non binary people in the dublin music scene.
  • The truth about Niall Blaise @ the soundhouse 15/01/24.
  • Emigrate release gig, Martina and the Moons, Sin é. 10/12/23.

    • Artists I manage


    Martina and the Moons

    All female four-piece band based in Dublin. In Februrary, they supported Belfast band Pinkslips at Sin E. Band consists of Martina Moon (vocals, songwriting), Sarah Morgan (guitar), Aisling Stephans McGowan (bass), and Zahira Ellis (drums). Their debut single "Emigrate" has accumulated over 3000 streamson Spotify, and has been featured in publications such as GCN Magazine and The Going Out Guide.

    Listen on Spotify

    Niall Bliase

    Niall Blaise is a singer/ songwriter based in Dublin. His music is full of 80’s pop rock splashed with a modern take of 00’s pop. His main inspirations come from artists such as Blondie, Slayyyter, and Rina Sawayama. You can expect this show to be full of glamorous pop bangers from this diva that will get you ready for the biggest debut show of your life. The crowd will not fail to be entranced by the stage presence of this trail blazing pop diva as he takes to the stage
